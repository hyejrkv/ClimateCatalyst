What can We Do?

Simple steps to unlock these co-benefits in our own lives.

Active Travel

Choose walking or cycling for short trips. It improves air quality and boosts your daily physical activity.

Home Efficiency

Improve insulation or switch to heat pumps. This reduces dampness and keeps your home healthy and warm.

Eat Green

Shift towards a plant-based diet. Sustainable food choices significantly reduce agricultural ammonia emissions.

Speak Up

Support local climate policies. Your voice helps local authorities implement co-benefit projects faster.
